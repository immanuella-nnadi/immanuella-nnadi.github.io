About Me

IN

I'm a data analyst passionate about helping businesses make sense of their data. I enjoy transforming complex, messy datasets into clear stories and actionable insights — most recently turning 21,000+ food delivery records into a five-page executive dashboard that surfaced real, usable recommendations for business expansion.

My focus is on building dashboards that actually get used, not just admired — reporting that speaks to how a business runs, not just what the numbers say. My background in Business Administration shapes how I approach analysis: I think about what the numbers mean for the business, and how to present them so stakeholders can act on them. Time spent in marketing and communications sharpened that instinct further.

When I'm not working with data, you'll find me on a volleyball court, with a book, or working on content.
